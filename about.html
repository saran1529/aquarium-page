Freshwater Aquarium Fishes

These are ideal for beginners and are the most common in aquariums.

S.No FISH DETAILS
1 GUPPY Easy to care for, vibrant colors, and livebearers.
Hardy fish that thrive in community tanks.
Tank size: Minimum 5 gallons.
2 MOLLY Peaceful and great for community tanks.
Can adapt to brackish or freshwater setups.
Tank size: Minimum 10 gallons.
3 BETTA Stunning, colorful fins.
Best kept alone or with non-aggressive tankmates.
Tank size: Minimum 5 gallons.
4 TETRA Small, peaceful schooling fish.
Prefer to be in groups of 6 or more.
Tank size: Minimum 10 gallons.
5 SHRIMPS Shrimps are sensitive to fluctuations, so ensure consistent pH, temperature, and ammonia/nitrite levels.
Live plants like Java Moss, Anubias, or Dwarf Hairgrass provide hiding spots and biofilm.
Avoid aggressive fish like Bettas or Cichlids; peaceful fish like Neon Tetras are ideal.
Provide plenty of hiding spaces like caves and moss for baby shrimps (shrimplets).
Feed high-quality shrimp pellets, algae wafers, and blanched vegetables like zucchini or spinach.
6 SNAILS Assassin Snails feed on smaller snails such as Pond Snails, Ramshorn Snails, and Malaysian Trumpet Snails.
They lay individual eggs on hard surfaces like plants, rocks, or tank decorations.
Peaceful toward tankmates, including fish, shrimp, and larger snails.
